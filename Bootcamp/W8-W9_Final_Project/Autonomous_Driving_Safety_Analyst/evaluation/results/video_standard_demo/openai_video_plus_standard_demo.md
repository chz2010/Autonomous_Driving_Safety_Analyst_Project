- **SOTIF Limitation:** Highly relevant. The failure arises from the system's inability to handle edge cases due to functional insufficiencies and performance limitations.

### Standards Interpretation
- **ISO 26262:** Focuses on E/E system faults, which are not the primary cause here. However, it emphasizes the need for robust safety goals and verification processes to mitigate risks from malfunctioning behavior.
- **ISO 21448 (SOTIF):** Addresses the insufficiencies in intended functionality, emphasizing the need to identify and mitigate risks from edge cases and performance limitations (ISO 21448:2022(E), Clause 26).
- **ISO 8800:** Highlights the importance of data coverage, model robustness, and AI lifecycle controls to ensure safety in AI-enabled functions (ISO 8800 Evaluation Scheme, Clause purpose).
